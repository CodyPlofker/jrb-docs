Variation 04 · Isolate curation

V3 carousel mechanics applied across three merchandising rows

Keeps V3's carousel + stars + reviews + price + NEW badge. The single change: one featured row becomes three rows — Bestsellers, New Arrivals, and Bobbi's Picks — each with its own horizontal carousel. This tests whether showing multiple curation lenses drives more engagement than a single row, and gives us directional signal (via row-level click share) on which curation type customers prefer. A clean A/B on curation type is Phase 2.
