Frequently asked questions

What is the Blushing Stick?

A ceramide-rich, cream-to-powder blush in stick form. Apply directly from the stick and blend with fingers for a natural, lit-from-within flush that lasts all day.

How many shades are there?

Nine shades ranging from warm peach to deep berry, designed to work on every skin tone from fair to deep.

Can I use it on my lips too?

Yes. The Blushing Stick is designed for both cheeks and lips, giving you a monochromatic, effortless look with one product.

Which shade is Joy?

Joy is a warm rosy pink — the signature shade Emily co-created with Bobbi Brown. It's designed to look like a natural, healthy flush on all skin tones.

Is it clean? What's in the formula?

Absolutely. Every Jones Road product is clean, cruelty-free, and free of parabens, sulfates, and phthalates. The Blushing Stick is ceramide-rich for added moisture and skin benefits.
