Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Blushing Stick?

A creamy, buildable lip and cheek tint that gives you a natural-looking flush with a skin-loving formula. Apply directly and blend with your fingers for a seamless, effortless look.

How do I choose my shade?

For a natural look, choose a shade close to the color of your lips or the flush of your cheeks. Joy and Pretty are great for fair skin, Bare and Chic for medium, and Flush, Cheeky, or Noir for deeper tones.

Can I use it on both lips and cheeks?

Yes. The Blushing Stick is designed as a dual-use product for both lips and cheeks. The formula is hydrating enough for lips and blendable enough for cheeks.

Is the formula clean?

Yes. Like all Jones Road products, the Blushing Stick is made with clean ingredients including jojoba oil and vitamin E. Free from parabens, sulfates, phthalates, and synthetic fragrances.

How long does it last?

The cream-to-powder formula sets and lasts through a full day of wear. For extra longevity, set with a light dusting of translucent powder.
