Key Takeaways

  • Listicles are 58% of Jurni revenue — build the listicle Shopify template first. EyeshadowStick listicles alone are $152K/mo.
  • Trojan Horse is bigger than expected at $120K — Pinterest HeroKit ($52K) and HeroKit Moms ($21K) are major drivers via AppLovin. These were previously undercounted.
  • Creator pages are efficient — best CVR (1.40%) and RPS ($1.20) despite only 8 funnels. Emily DiDonato drives 83% of creator revenue.
  • Multi-Product has the worst CVR (0.80%) — consider merging into the listicle template with multi-product ATC functionality.
  • WTFGeneric is 97% of Generic revenue — one page to migrate, rest are low-traffic utility pages.
