How to read this board

Four rows = four ownership lanes: Product Dev (ongoing background), Product Marketing (Katie · Phases 1–2), Marketing (Cody + channel leads · Phases 3, 5), Creative (Phases 4–5). Time flows left-to-right across five color-coded phases. Follow numbered badges 1 → 18 to read chronologically — work hops between rows as ownership changes.

GTM handoff happens between Phase 2 and Phase 3 — Final Name (Step 7) locks, then Marketing picks up with Launch Surveys (Step 8). PD keeps running in parallel the whole time, delivering approved product at T–10.

Click any step to open its detail panel — includes inputs, deliverables, related skills, and an example slot you can link or upload to.

Tier scoping (set at Step 10 · /launch-brief)

Tier 1Hero launch. TV/CTV · Homepage takeover · LP · 30–50 ad concepts · 8+ emails · 4-week organic calendar.
Tier 2Major launch. No TV · LP · 20–30 ads · 5–6 emails · 2–3 week organic.
Tier 3Standard. PDP + minimal homepage · 10–15 ads · 3–4 emails · 2-week organic.
Tier 4Restock / quiet. PDP only · 5 ads max · 1 email · Reactive organic.

Feedback loop

Post-launch learnings (Steps 15–18) flow back upstream: CX + review signals to PD · Meta analysis to Creative · CRO learnings to next launch's Strategic Brief. The jrb-customer-intelligence repo gets merged on every launch — each new launch starts smarter than the last.
