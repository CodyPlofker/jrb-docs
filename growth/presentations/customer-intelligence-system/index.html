Simulate Research

Instead of running a survey for every question (which takes weeks and costs money), we can now simulate customer responses using everything we already know.

It's not a replacement for real research — it's a speed tool for getting a directional answer in minutes instead of weeks.

Example

How it works in practice

"Would customers prefer a mini kit or full-size bundle for holiday gifting?"
Typeform surveys 98.1% want Mini Miracle Balms available year-round
ListenLabs interviews 85% of prospects cite "trying for the first time" as key motivation
OuterSignal purchases Holiday AOV spikes 23% — customers are willing to spend more during gifting season
Richpanel tickets "Gift set" and "gift for mom" queries spike every Nov-Dec

Answer: THINK (high directional confidence)

~65-75% would prefer a curated mini kit over a full-size bundle for gifting. Our top 2 personas (54% of revenue) both index on curation and giftability over raw value. Recommendation: Move forward with mini kit concept, validate with a small test.
