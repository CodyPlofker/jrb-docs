Numbered Callouts — Survey-Backed Decisions

V1 Additions
  • Star + review count below title. Survey Q3: 25% cite star rating pre-click; "glaring zero" is a documented conversion killer.
  • "Includes:" subtitle on bundles — primary, not secondary. Survey Q8: 77% of respondents rank what's-inside as most important (vs 13% total savings).
V2 Additions
  • Tap-to-preview hint on mobile; hover on desktop. Survey Q9: 65% chose this as their #1 tile upgrade.
  • Tappable shade swatches (5 + overflow). 86% need shade info pre-click (Q3); MAC cited unprompted in Q10 for swatches on 3 skin tones.
  • Save $X on bundles = secondary, NOT primary. Respects the 6:1 contents-over-savings ratio.
V3 Additions
  • Trust strip above the grid. 17% clean-beauty recall — answers "why buy here" pre-scroll.
  • Stock-state indicators + inline "Notify Me" button. 188 Richpanel OOS tickets/60d; survey Q4 requested restock dates unprompted.
  • "Showing all" in count — no "see all" click. Q11 verbatim: "You are going to lose significant sales on items/lines not displayed."
  • Expanded chips: New + Best-Sellers surfaced alongside categories. Concern/undertone filters in the filter drawer.
Mobile-Specific Choices
  • 2-column grid maintained — current production pattern, confirmed working.
  • 14px minimum for price (currently 13px — raised to prevent mobile truncation).
  • Swatches are 16px visual / 28px touch area per Baymard 7mm minimum.
  • Sticky sub-nav chips — horizontally scrollable, first-chip active visible.
  • No sidebar filters — filter/sort stays as drawer toggles in the top bar.
