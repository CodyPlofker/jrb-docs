Mijo YT Ads — Round 1

9 ads reviewed · 60s · 16×9 · Concepts 1 (A/B/C), 2 (A/B/C), 3 (A/B/C)

Overall / Pattern-Level Feedback

Recurring issues across most ads

  • Pacing is too slow. Bobbi's delivery drags, edits linger, and most ads take 15s to get into the concept. Target 5–10s max before the hook pays off.
  • Audio gaps between Bobbi's lines. Long silences before, between, and after her lines make it feel scripted and cheesy. Tighten so audio flows continuously.
  • Bobbi's credibility is never established. Every ad assumes the viewer knows her — we can't. Add a quick identifier (lower-third, VO, or on-screen text): "Bobbi Brown — founder of Jones Road Beauty, founder of Bobbi Brown Cosmetics, world-renowned makeup artist."
  • Hooks are too soft for prospecting. Don't lead with features/benefits or brand/product names. Hooks should be about the viewer — problem, desire, or curiosity.
  • Music feels off for JRB. Needs review across all ads.
  • Too branded, not DR enough. These feel closer to TV spots than performance assets. See CTV opportunity below.

Erin Action items

  • Weigh in on visuals for YT 2C's "Not Your Skin" sequence (see 2C below).

CTV opportunity
