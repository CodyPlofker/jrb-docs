FAQ

Every question we get, answered.

Is Lip Recharge tinted or sheer?

Lip Recharge delivers buildable sheer-to-medium color with a glossy finish — think luminous, never heavy. All 7 shades are wearable across every skin tone; some (like Petal and Me) are sheer enough to work as everyday no-makeup makeup, while Dahlia and Sangria deliver real pigment with a single swipe.

How long does one tube last?

With daily use, most customers finish a tube in 3–4 months. The balm formula means you use less per application than a traditional lipstick.

Is this safe during pregnancy?

Yes. Lip Recharge is formulated without parabens, phthalates, mineral oil, or synthetic fragrance. All our shades are dermatologist-tested and free from the 2,700+ ingredients we've eliminated.

Will this work on mature lips?

Absolutely — this is one of our most popular products with customers 50+. The buttery balm base conditions dry, thinning lips while the sheer pigment gives back the color mature lips often lose. It won't bleed or settle into lines.

Can I wear this over lip liner?

Yes — pair with our Lip Pencil for lasting color and defined edges. The Lip Pencil in Nude Pink is the most popular pairing with Petal and Me.

What's your return policy?

Free and easy returns within 30 days, even on opened shades. We want you to find your perfect match.
