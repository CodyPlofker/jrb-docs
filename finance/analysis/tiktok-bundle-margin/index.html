METHODOLOGY
  • COGS = NetSuite weighted-average cost (averagecost) on the FG Assembly SKU. Includes raws, fill, packaging, assembly. WIP/test/PROMO SKUs filtered out.
  • Gross Margin = Sell Price − COGS · Landed GM = Gross − TikTok fee − Creator commission · Contribution = Landed − Fulfillment − Returns reserve.
  • Color thresholds (configurable): Green ≥ GM target. Yellow = GM target − 20pts. Red below that.
  • Excludes: ad spend, talent fees, content production, customer service. Add above the line for fully-loaded margin.
