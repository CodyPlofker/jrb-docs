Interpretation

The Jan-to-Feb decline (58% for Dusty Rose, 53% for Sunkissed) is normal post-launch settling. But the Feb-to-Mar collapse (97.5% and 86%) is far too steep to be organic. Products don't lose 97.5% of demand in one month unless they're out of stock or delisted. This is likely unmet demand.
