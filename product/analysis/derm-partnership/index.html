Section 04

What This Enables

A derm partnership isn't just credibility. It unlocks five concrete capabilities JRB doesn't have today.

1
Proprietary Ingredient Framework
"Bobbi's Skin Prep Complex" co-developed with Dr. [X]. Tatcha has Hadasei-3, Tula has S6Pro, Charlotte Tilbury has Recoverstem. JRB currently has nothing.
2
Clinical Claims
"Clinically shown to improve skin texture in 4 weeks" backed by real testing. Closes the credibility gap between JRB and clinical-first competitors.
3
Infinite Content Engine
Bobbi + derm conversations about "what actually works." YouTube, Instagram, email, podcast. The skeptic + the scientist is an endlessly compelling format.
4
Move the "Worth Paying More For" Needle
Tracksuit shows JRB at 38% vs. 44% category average on "worth paying more for." A derm co-sign adds perceived value without raising prices.
5
Own "Expert Skin Prep" with Clinical Teeth
Without a derm, it's a marketing claim. With a derm, it's clinically validated positioning. The difference between saying it and proving it.
Section 05

Who NOT to Pursue

Criteria for disqualification. Not every visible derm is the right derm.

  • TikTok mega-derms (2M+ followers) — entertainment-first, wrong demo, wrong tone for JRB's consumer
  • Dr. Muneeb Shah — despite NYC base, Remedy at Target + Gen Z audience = wrong fit
  • Sephora-exclusive or brand-exclusive derms — Bhanusali at Rhode, Idriss at Sephora
  • Non-MDs — estheticians are a weaker credibility signal (see Merit example)
  • LA-based dermatologists — unless JRB is expanding beyond its NYC positioning
The Principle
JRB needs a derm who feels like a peer to Bobbi — someone with decades of practice, a no-BS philosophy, and zero interest in becoming a beauty influencer. The derm validates. Bobbi leads.

Section 07

Next Steps

Four steps to move from research to first conversations.

Step 1
Internal Alignment
Present concept to Bobbi and leadership. Confirm appetite for a named derm partnership vs. anonymous advisory board. Get alignment on the "Chief Skin Science Officer" model.
Step 2
Outreach to Tier 1
Exploratory conversations with Dr. Engelman, Dr. Zeichner, and Dr. Marmur. Gauge interest, availability, and philosophical fit. Understand their expectations on creative control.
Step 3
Define the Role
Formalize title, time commitment (5–15 hrs/month), equity structure (3–5%), exclusivity terms, and content expectations. Draft a partnership term sheet.
Step 4
Test with CAB
Add 2–3 derm credibility questions to the next Customer Advisory Board survey. Validate that "dermatologist-developed" moves the needle for JRB's core consumer.
