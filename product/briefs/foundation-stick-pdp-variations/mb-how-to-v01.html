How To Apply

Four steps to your best skin

Miracle Balm is not a foundation. It is a skin-first tint that melts into your complexion. Here is how to use it.

1

Warm It Up

Scoop a small amount and rub between your fingertips for 5 seconds. This melts the balm so it blends seamlessly.

2

Press and Blend

Press onto cheeks, forehead, and chin. Use patting motions, never rub. Your body heat does the work.

3

Build Your Coverage

Start sheer and add more where you want it. One layer for a natural glow, two for soft color.

4

Multi-Use Magic

Use as blush on cheeks, highlighter on cheekbones, and tint on lips. One product, three looks.
