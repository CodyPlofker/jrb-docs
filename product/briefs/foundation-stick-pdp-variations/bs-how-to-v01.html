Blushing Stick — How-To — V01: 2x2 Step Grid

How to Apply

Apply, blend, done

Four simple steps to a natural, buildable flush. No tools, no brushes, no mirror required.

01

Swipe

Swipe Blushing Stick directly onto the apple of your cheek. One single stroke is all you need.

02

Blend

Use your fingertips to blend upward toward your cheekbone. The warmth of your fingers melts it into skin.

03

Build (Optional)

Want more color? Add a second swipe and blend again. Each layer adds dimension without heaviness.

04

Done

That is it. No setting, no powder, no touch-ups. Blushing Stick stays put all day.
