How to Use

Genius in a pot. Once you break the seal of the balm, you can wear it alone, or use it to bronze, highlight, or tint the skin by mixing and matching shades.

1
Break the Seal
Before your first use, press into the top of the balm with your index finger to release the pigment.
2
Scoop a Small Amount
Pick up a small, pea-sized amount and warm it between your fingers to soften the formula.
3
Dab Onto Skin
Using your fingers or The Everything Brush, dab onto face, lips, or anywhere you want to tint or glow.

*Miracle Balm is not intended to be used on the eyes or eyelids
4
Blend It In
Blend well and layer with other makeup products or wear alone for a natural, no-makeup makeup look.

What Is Miracle Balm?

A Cream Blush for Lips & Cheeks

Not a lip balm. Not a tinted moisturizer. Miracle Balm is a cream-to-powder formula that melts into skin, delivering a natural flush of buildable color. One product replaces your blush, bronzer, highlighter, and lip tint.

  • Multi-use: cheeks, eyes, lips, and body
  • No brushes needed — apply with fingertips in 30 seconds
  • Buildable from sheer glow to bold color
  • Clean formula with jojoba oil and vitamin E
