Jojoba Seed Oil: your skin's perfect match

Jojoba oil is the closest plant oil to human sebum. That means the Blushing Stick absorbs like it belongs on your skin, not like it's sitting on top.

  • Absorbs without clogging pores or leaving residue
  • Keeps the cream formula blendable for minutes
  • Naturally non-comedogenic and soothing for sensitive skin
  • Helps color melt into skin for a natural finish

Also formulated with: Vitamin E, Shea Butter, Natural Pigments
