How to Apply

Four steps to your best skin

What The Foundation feels different because it is different. Here is how to get the most out of every application.

Step 01

Warm it up

Warm a pea-sized amount between your fingertips for 5 seconds. The heat from your hands activates the formula.

Step 02

Press and blend

Press and blend into skin — start at center, work outward. Use your fingers, not a brush. The warmth makes all the difference.

Step 03

Build your coverage

Build coverage where needed — under eyes, around nose. The formula is buildable without looking cakey.

Step 04

Let it set

Set with Just A Sec spray or let it self-set in 2 minutes. The initial tackiness disappears as the product bonds with your skin.

"The initial tackiness is the product bonding with your skin. It disappears in 2 minutes."
