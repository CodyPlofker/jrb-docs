Hero Ingredient

Hyaluronic acid: the moisture magnet inside your foundation

Most foundations sit on top of skin. Ours works with it. Hyaluronic acid is a naturally occurring molecule that binds water to skin cells, creating deep hydration that lasts from morning to evening.

Combined with squalane and vitamin E, it transforms What The Foundation from coverage into actual skincare — a tinted moisture balm that improves your skin while you wear it.
