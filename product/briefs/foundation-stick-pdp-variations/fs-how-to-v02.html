Foundation Stick — How To Apply — V02: 4-Step Progression

How to Apply

30 seconds. Done.

Step 1

Prep

Start with clean, moisturized skin. That's it.

Step 2

Swipe

Swipe directly onto skin wherever you want coverage. Cheeks, forehead, chin.

Step 3

Blend

Blend with fingers or The Face Blending Brush in short outward strokes.

Step 4

Build

Add another pass only where you want more. Medium to full, still like skin.
