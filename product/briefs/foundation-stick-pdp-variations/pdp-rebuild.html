How to Use

Break the seal by twisting open the compact. Scoop a small amount with your fingertip — a little goes a long way. Dab onto cheeks, lips, or eyelids and blend outward with your fingers until you reach your desired coverage.

Layer for more intensity or mix two shades for a custom color. For a dewy highlight, apply Golden Hour or Magic Hour to the high points of your face.

What is Miracle Balm?
Miracle Balm is a multi-use cream color for cheeks, lips, eyes, and body. It's a cream blush — not a lip balm — that gives your skin a natural, lit-from-within glow with just your fingertips. No brushes needed.
How do I find my shade?
Take our shade quiz or send a selfie to hi@jonesroadbeauty.com and our team will match you. You can also try the mini ($22) to test your shade before going full size.
