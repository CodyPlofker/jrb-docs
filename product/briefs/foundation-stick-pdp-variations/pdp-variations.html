Section 16: Undertone Guide — 64.1% want undertone guidance with visuals
16.1 — Visual cool/warm/neutral comparison

Undertone Guide

Not sure if you're warm, cool, or neutral?

Warm

Veins appear greenish. Gold jewelry looks best. Skin has yellow, peachy, or golden tones.

Best shades: Golden, Peach, Honey

Cool

Veins appear bluish. Silver jewelry looks best. Skin has pink or reddish tones.

Best shades: Dusty Rose, Rose

Neutral

Veins appear both blue and green. Both gold and silver work. Skin has balanced tones.

Best shades: Magic Hour, Tawny
