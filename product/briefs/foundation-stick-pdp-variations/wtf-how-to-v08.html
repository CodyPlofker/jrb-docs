What The Foundation — How-To — V08: Carousel Steps

How to Apply

Four steps. Two minutes. Done.

Step 01 of 04

Warm it in your hands

Warm a pea-sized amount between your fingertips for 5 seconds. Body heat transforms the balm from solid to silky.

Step 02 of 04

Press and blend outward

Start at center, work outward. Use your fingers, not a brush — the warmth from your hands activates the formula.

Step 03 of 04

Build where you need it

Under eyes, around nose. The formula is buildable without looking heavy or cakey.

Step 04 of 04

Wait 2 minutes and go

The initial tackiness is the product bonding with your skin. It disappears completely as the formula sets.
