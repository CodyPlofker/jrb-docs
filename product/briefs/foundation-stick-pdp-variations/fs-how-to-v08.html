Foundation Stick — How-To — V08: Carousel Steps

Swipe Through

Your 30-second routine, step by step

Swipe through each application stage. The entire process takes less time than reading this sentence.

Step 1 of 4

Draw

10 Seconds

Draw 3 short lines on each cheek, forehead, nose, and chin directly from the stick. Light pressure, quick strokes.

Step 2 of 4

Blend

10 Seconds

Use fingertips to blend outward. Your body heat melts the formula into your skin for a seamless, second-skin finish.

Step 3 of 4

Build

10 Seconds

Add a second pass only where you want more coverage. The formula layers beautifully without caking or heaviness.

Step 4 of 4

Go

0 Seconds

No setting spray or powder needed. Cap it, toss it in your bag, and go. Lasts 12+ hours.
