Foundation Stick — Ingredients — V05: In vs Out

The Formula

What's in. What's out.

What's In It

  • Ceramide NP

    Strengthens your skin barrier while you wear it.

  • Squalane

    Locks in moisture. Coverage never goes dry.

  • Shea Butter

    Creamy glide-on texture — never heavy or waxy.

  • Sodium Hyaluronate

    Deep, lasting hydration from morning to night.

What's Not In It

  • Talc

    No powder fillers that settle into lines.

  • Heavy Waxes

    Nothing that drags or feels like a mask.

  • Synthetic Dyes

    Pigment from minerals, not lab colors.

  • Parabens

    Clean preservation, no exceptions.

  • Mineral Oil

    Skin-feeding oils only.
